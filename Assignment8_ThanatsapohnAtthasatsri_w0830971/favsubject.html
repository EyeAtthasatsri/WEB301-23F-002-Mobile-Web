Third,

My current focus is on the Mobile Web, my third favorite subject this semester. Learning about mobile-friendly layouts, touch interactions, and optimizing performance for diverse devices.

Final favorite subject

Last semester, I enjoyed studying Web Usability, my favorite class. It's all about making websites and apps that people find easy to use. I learned to focus on what users need and create designs that not only look good but are also simple to navigate. We did projects where we made wireframes, like blueprints for websites. This helped me learn how to plan where things go on a website to make it user-friendly. This class gave me important skills to make digital stuff easy to use and accessible, highlighting how make users happy in the design process.
